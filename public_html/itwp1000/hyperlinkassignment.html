7 Stanzas

by Eman D

Because of you, I cant say "I Love You" anymore.
My heart was locked but some how you opened the door.
No ones ever made me feel the way you have.
Things will never be the same, nothing can replace what we had.

We joked all the time, laughed endlessly.
Your an angel and I fell in love helplessly.
Sometimes you'd cry but I was sure to be there.
Nothing less of beautiful, sometimes all I could do was stare.

Your smile, I loved your smile.
Your steeze, no one came close to your style.
Gorgeous and beautiful, not "sexy" or "fine".
A heart so generous enough to warm mine.

Things are a mess, I was being selfish.
You were everything I wanted, my every wish.
I had to let you go, but I regret it every single day.
If I knew things would be like this, I would've stayed.

Now, I'm scared to say "I Love You" to anyone else,
because your love was the purest feeling I've ever felt.
I don't wanna hear "I Love You" from no one other than you.
Our love is lost, theres nothing we can do.

Every night, tears slide down my blank face.
My body so cold, just wanting you to embrace.
I lie on my bed, w/ feelings of 1,000 pounds on my chest.
Waking from my dreams of you, never can I rest.

7 stanzas aren't even a fraction of what I want to say.
There will never be a night or day, where I will truly be OK.
Say it once more, say it for me just one more time.
"I Love You" from you lips is all I wanna hear before I die.
